Inventracker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 06 June 2026

What we collect

Nothing. Inventracker is a local-first app. All your inventory, customer, invoice, payment, and stock-movement data is stored only on your device, in private app storage that other apps cannot access. Backup files you create or share are encrypted before they leave your device.

We do not collect, transmit, store, or have access to any of this data. No analytics, no telemetry, no usage tracking is sent from the app.

Network use

Inventracker makes outbound network requests in only two situations:

  1. Currency conversion (optional). When you manually change the app's currency and choose to convert existing prices, the app queries a public exchange-rate API (Frankfurter, open.er-api.com, or exchangerate.host) to fetch the current rate. Only the two currency codes (e.g. "EUR" and "USD") are sent. No personal or business data is included in the request.
  2. Subscription status check (every app launch). See the next section.

All other operations — invoices, transfers, reports, backups — are performed entirely offline on your device.

Billing and subscription data

If you purchase Inventracker Pro, payments are processed by Google Play and verified through RevenueCat, Inc. (San Francisco, CA).

Each time you open the app, the RevenueCat SDK sends an anonymous app-generated identifier to check whether you have an active subscription. No inventory, customer, or invoice data is transmitted. If you purchase Pro, the SDK additionally transmits your purchase token and entitlement status to verify the purchase.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.

Backups and exports

When you create a backup or export a file (CSV, JSON, PDF, HTML), the file is generated on your device and shared via your operating system's standard share sheet. You choose the destination. Backup files are encrypted on your device before sharing.

Permissions

Inventracker requests Android permissions only for the features that need them:

  • Camera — only when you actively use the barcode scanner. Images are not saved or sent.
  • Internet — for the optional currency rate fetch and the subscription status check described above.
  • Storage — only when you save backups or import files you select.

Permissions are not used for any other purpose.

Children's privacy

Inventracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone — including children — as described above.
