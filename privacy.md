[**Inventracker — Privacy Policy**]

**Effective date:** 06/06/2026

**What we collect:**
Nothing. Inventracker is a local-only app. All your inventory, customer, invoice, payment, and stock-movement data is stored only on your device, encrypted at rest.
We do not collect, transmit, store, or have access to any of this data. No analytics, no telemetry, no usage tracking is sent from the app.

**Network use:**
Inventracker makes outbound network requests only when you manually change the app's currency and choose to convert existing prices.
In that case, the app queries a public exchange-rate API (Frankfurter, open.er-api.com, or exchangerate.host) to fetch the current rate.
Only the two currency codes (e.g. "EUR" and "USD") are sent. No personal or business data is included in the request.
All other operations — invoices, transfers, reports, backups — are performed entirely offline on your device.

**Backups and exports:**
When you create a backup or export a file (CSV, JSON, PDF, HTML), the file is generated on your device and shared via your operating system's standard share sheet.
You choose the destination. Backups are encrypted on your device before sharing.

**Permissions:**
Inventracker requests Android permissions only for the features that need them: Camera (only when you actively use the barcode scanner; no images are saved or sent), 
Internet (only for the optional currency rate fetch described above), and Storage (only when you save backups or imports). Permissions are not used for any other purpose.

**Children's privacy:** Inventracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone — including children — as described above.
